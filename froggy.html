Froggy

#Illustration #Graphic #Sketch

Illustration inspired by the biodiversity of Colombia and what meaning this animal has for our culture

The spiritual meaning of the frog is very broad, since it is an animal that is full of lessons for those who own it. On the one hand, the frog teaches you to adapt well to changes, to see them with a certain humor, even those that seem negative. When a change comes into your life, it's for a reason: a new cycle ends because a new one begins.
